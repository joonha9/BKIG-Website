About Us

Boston Korea Investment Group — connecting Seoul & Boston.

Founded in 2023

Connecting Korean Finance Globally

Based in Boston, BKIG is a Korean investment group dedicated to fostering a global financial ecosystem that bridges Seoul and Boston. We bring together students and professionals with a shared passion for finance, creating a platform for learning, networking, and real-world investment experience.

Our mission is to connect Korean finance globally—through rigorous equity research, hands-on portfolio management, and a strong alumni network across top firms in both the United States and Korea. BKIG serves as the bridge where tradition meets innovation, and where the next generation of financial leaders is built.
