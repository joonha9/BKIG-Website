1. Scope

This Privacy Policy describes how Boston Korea Investment Group (“BKIG”) collects, uses, and protects information when you visit our website or use our services. By using this website, you consent to the practices described in this policy.

We may collect information you provide directly, such as name, email address, and school affiliation when you apply to join BKIG or contact us. We may also automatically collect certain technical information when you visit our website, including IP address, browser type, and usage data.

Access to the BKIG Terminal and certain member areas may require authentication. Account credentials and usage within those areas are processed in accordance with this policy and our Terms of Use.

3. How We Use Information

We use the information we collect to operate our organization, communicate with members and applicants, improve our website and services, and comply with legal obligations. We do not sell your personal information to third parties. We may share information with service providers who assist us in operating our website and activities, subject to confidentiality obligations.

4. Data Security

We implement appropriate technical and organizational measures to protect your personal information against unauthorized access, alteration, disclosure, or destruction. No method of transmission over the Internet or electronic storage is completely secure; we encourage you to use strong credentials and to contact us if you have concerns about your data.

5. Contact

For questions about this Privacy Policy or our data practices, please contact us at contact@bkig.org or through the contact form on our website.
